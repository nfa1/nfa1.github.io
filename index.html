Evaluation of Web Graphics

  • Do the graphics on the page serve a purpose? If so, what is it? If not, why not?
    • The graphics on the page add visual/aesthetic appeal to the information on the web page, helping to maintain viewer interest and attention
    • They also help to create visual structure for information and links on the page
    • They also communicate or explain ideas visually
  • Do the graphics on the page meet the guidelines outlined in the “Purpose and Guidelines for Effective Use of Web Graphics” article? If not, which guidelines were not met?
    • The graphics on the page do meet the guidelines outlined in the “Purpose and Guidelines for Effective Use of Web Graphics” article
